# Doggy's House: Your Pet's Home Away From Home

Discover Doggy's House, the ultimate platform for pet reservations, managing a variety of pet-friendly hotels. Whether it's a weekend getaway or an extended stay, we've got your furry friends covered. Make your pet's experience extraordinary with Doggy's House.

## Technical Overview

Doggy's House is a well-crafted platform designed to make pet reservations seamless and enjoyable. Here's a glimpse into the technical aspects that power this innovative solution:

### Technologies Used

- **Node with Sequelize ORM:** The server-side magic is powered by Node, while Sequelize serves as the ORM, ensuring efficient and secure interactions with the database.

- **Vite and Tailwind for the Frontend:** The frontend is built using Vite for speed and Tailwind for a sleek and responsive user interface.

- **MySQL as the Database:** The robust MySQL database is the foundation for managing pet-related information, reservations, and user data.

- **MercadoPago Integration:** Simplify payment processes with our integration with MercadoPago, providing a secure and seamless payment platform for users.

### Other Features

- **Dynamic Reservation System:** Our platform allows for easy and dynamic pet reservations across a network of diverse pet-friendly hotels.

- **User-Friendly Interface:** A thoughtfully designed interface ensures a smooth and enjoyable experience for users navigating the platform.

- **Scalable Architecture:** The use of Node and Sequelize ensures a scalable architecture, accommodating the growth of both users and pet-friendly establishments.
